JOIN OUR COMMUNITY

Appropriate candidates for Maybach Academy exhibit the following characteristics:

Diagnosis of cognitive delay or developmental disability (low to low-average IQ)
No history of significant behavioral or psychological disorders
Basic verbal skills or ability to use alternate communication device or strategy independently
Ability to work within a small group and to transition between school and community activities
Motivated to work hard towards a goal of independence
Family network committed to working with the school

Tuition Information

Tuition to Maybach Academy is $25,100 for Middle and High School students. State grants from $9,000-$24,000 are available for special needs students to help cover tuition.

The Post-Secondary Bridges program for young adults has a half day option starting at $11,000.

Payment plans, scholarships and discounts are available. Limited need-based financial aid is available. Eligibility is determined by the FACTS Grant and Aid Assessment Program.

Maybach Academy does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, gender, religious, ethnic or national origin in the administration of its educational programs, admissions policies, financial aid policies, or employment practices.
